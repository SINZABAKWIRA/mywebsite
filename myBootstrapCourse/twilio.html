There are endless resources on learning and teaching code,
it can be difficult to know which will both engage your students, and prepare them for jobs in industry. Sometimes it feels easier to create your own materials, but keeping your syllabus up to date with industry trends is a full time job.

TwilioQuest strong is a PC-roleplaying game that teaches
foundational programming skills, from popular languages and their development environments, to understanding web services. Embark on the Fog Owl with your students, and explore The Cloud, rewarding exploration of programming topics through roleplaying game mechanics.

TwilioQuest for Education gives you everything you need to bring TwilioQuest to your classroom, including lesson plans, exercises, and a community of innovative and supportive educators. Twilio is committed to helping create the next generation of developers, and provide TwilioQuest for Education for free to educators.

MISTY II IN THE ROLE OF CEDRIC

We selected Misty II, the first professional-grade platform robot, as our inspiration for
Cedric, our NPC (non-player character) guide in TwilioQuest.
Cedric lives in a cloud but inhabits the body of Misty II in the real world as his Avatar.

  • Learn to program alongside twilioQuest
  • 3D-atttachments for misty II
  • Experiment with facial recogonition
  • Use Arduino to bluid robote application (Skills)

Misty is flexible and engaging for students of all interests. With Misty II (Cedric) in the classroom, you’ll see the excitement about robots turn into a passion for coding and learning about technologies that are transforming our world.
